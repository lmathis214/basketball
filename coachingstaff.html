Coaching Staff



Michael Wantz

Varsity Head Coach

Michael Wantz begins his 18th year as the Roncalli boys' basketball coach. He owns a career coaching record of 246-134 and became the school’s all-time leader in wins when the Rebels defeated Brebeuf in the 2009 Sectional championship game. He has led the Rebels to their only four sectional championships in school history, to double figure wins sixteen of his seventeen years, and to the school’s first conference championship in 2018.

Wantz is a 1993 graduate of Union City Community High School where he lettered in basketball, baseball and tennis. He received his bachelor’s degree from Ball State University where he was a student assistant with the men’s basketball team. Wantz has also served as an assistant coach at Wilberforce University and the University of Indianapolis.

Wantz has a master’s degree in curriculum and instruction from the University of Indianapolis and is currently teaching in the Business department at Roncalli. Michael and his wife Abby, have a son, Eli, and two daughters, Zoey and Piper.

Ian Brown

Varsity Assistant

Ian Brown begins his eighth year on the Rebel staff after 20 years of coaching experience at the collegiate level, with 10 as the head men's basketball coach at Doane College in Crete, Nebraska. He left Doane with the second-most wins in the program's history, coaching six players who earned an all-american status at the NAIA level and 25 NAIA Scholar-Athlete All-Americans. Prior to Doane, Brown was an assistant at Lincoln Memorial University (TN) and the University of Indianapolis. Brown is a teacher in the Physical Education and Health department and resides in Brownsburg with his two children, Cameryn and Crew.

John Hasty

Varsity Assistant

John Hasty begins his sixth year on the Rebel coaching staff. Coach Hasty graduated from Roncalli in 2008, and was a four year member of the basketball team. The record of the varsity the two years he was a part of the team was 34-8. He attended Marian University, playing a significant role in the Knights’ national championship football team. Hasty served as a graduate football assistant at Walsh University in North Canton, Ohio. He currently teaches World History in the Roncalli Social Studies department. He is married to Shelia Elsener (R ‘10) and they have one son, Luke, with another one due in March.

Jamin Wernke

Junior Varsity Head Coach

Jamin Wernke begins his 13th year on the Rebel coaching staff, and tenth as head junior varsity coach. Coach Wernke’s two previous coaching stops were at St. Barnabas and Morristown High School. He is a 2002 graduate of Roncalli High School, where he was a four-year member of the basketball program. Wernke is currently the athletic director at Holy Name and is married to Angela Grant (R ’03). Together they have two daughters, Avery and Anniston, and a son, Jimmy, as well as a fourth child on the way in late December.

Justin Agresta

Junior Varsity Assistant Coach

Justin Agresta is in his 16th season as a coach in the Rebel program, and his tenth as a junior varsity assistant coach. Agresta is a 2003 Roncalli grad and was a two-year starter for the Rebels, graduating as the school record holder for assists in a game. He attended the University of Indianapolis and is currently employed at Agresta, Storms & O’Leary, PC as an accountant. He and his wife Shawna reside on the southside of Indianapolis.

Justin Wernke

Freshmen Head Coach

Justin Wernke begins his 12th year on the Rebel coaching staff, and his tenth as the head freshmen coach. He played four years of high school basketball and was a starter on the 2006 Roncalli Sectional championship team. Wernke has also coached at St. Barnabas and is a sales rep at Piazza Produce. He and his wife, Lauren, welcomed their son, Justin James (JJ) in October.

Christoper Fey

Freshmen Assistant Coach

Christopher Fey is entering his second year as a freshman assistant coach. Coach Fey is a 2014 Roncalli graduate and was a four year member of the basketball program. He was an integral member of the 2013-14 team that defeated Warren Central in the sectional and battled state champion Tech in the sectional final. After Roncalli, Christopher attended Ball State University and is currently employed by Cityscape Residential.

LaDarius Mathis

Freshmen Assistant Coach

LaDarius Mathis is entering his first year as a freshmen assistant basketball coach, and his 15th overall. Coach Mathis graduated from Tyler Street Christian Academy in Dallas, Texas, where he earned All State honors in both football and basketball. He has coached football and basketball at various levels over the years and has also served in the United States Army for the last 8.5 years, where he was deployed in Afghanistan. Coach Mathis and his wife, Vanessa, have 4 children, 3 of whom attend Our Lady of the Greenwood..
